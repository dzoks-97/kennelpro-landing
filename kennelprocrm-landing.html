Features

Everything your kennel needs

Built specifically for professional dog breeders. One platform, complete control.

🐕
Dog Profiles

Complete profiles with photos, health records, vaccinations, documents, pedigrees and show results — all in one place.

🤝
B2B Transactions

Unique peer-to-peer sales system between registered breeders with real-time WebSocket confirmation workflow.

💰
Financial Reports

Track income, expenses and profit with monthly, yearly or custom period reports. Multi-currency support included.

🌳
Pedigree Management

Build and visualize 3-generation pedigrees with auto-fill from existing dogs in your system.

🐾
Litters & Puppies

Track litters from whelping through sale. Record individual puppy sales and generate per-litter financial reports.

🏆
Show Results

Record competition results, grades, CAC/CACIB titles and placements. Build an impressive portfolio of achievements.
